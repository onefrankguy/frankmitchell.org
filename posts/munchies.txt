### Background ###

Joe Talik's recipe from the _Mitchell Family Cookbook_.

### Ingredients ###

* 1 stick of butter
* 1 egg
* 0.5 cups sugar
* 1.25 cups flour
* 1 teaspoon baking powder
* 1 teaspoon vanilla
* 0.5 tablespoons salt
* 1 large jar of jam

### Preparation ###

Cream the butter and sugar. Mix all the ingredients together, except the jam. Make balls of dough, place on a greased cookie sheet and indent the center of each with your thumb. Fill the indentation with jam. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes.
