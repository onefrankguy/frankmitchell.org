### Background ###

This is my mother's pea salad (the one I remember form my childhood). It makes an excellent last minute "Oh my gosh I need something to bring to the party" food.

### Ingredients ###

* 1 10 ounce package of frozen peas, thawed
* 2 dill pickles, chopped fine
* 0.5 bell peppers, chopped fine
* 3 green onions, sliced
* 1 large tomato, chopped
* vinegar
* mayonnaise
* salt
* pepper

### Preparation ###

Mix all the ingredients together. Add vinegar, mayonnaise, salt, and pepper to taste.
