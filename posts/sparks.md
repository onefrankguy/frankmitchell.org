Slow, slow, slow, slow  
Don't want to take  
Things way too fast  

Sparks burn white bright  
And then they're gone  
Leave you holding ashes  

Detachment is the essence  
How do you cup  
Fire in my hands  

Between fingers light escapes  
Dance through the darkness  
Slow, slow, slow, slow
