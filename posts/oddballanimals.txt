#### Oddball Animals ####

Alligators attack agnostic  
Butterflies badgering bears  
Carrying crumbling crepes  
Dripping delectable damson  
Eatery edibles ergonomically  
From fastidiously fashioned  
Gourmet grapevine greenery  
Handmade hourly here  
In icicle igloos.
