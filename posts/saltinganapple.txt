#### Salting an Apple ####

Newton,  
invented Calculus,  
so that he might describe the Physical World  
in more specific terms  
than Hot  
and Wet  
and Cold  
and Dry.

Calculus,  
instructed Newton,  
so that he might manipulate Number  
between One  
and Two  
and Zero  
and One.
